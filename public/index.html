AI Usage Disclaimer

UDAYRA uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) only as a supporting and explanatory tool.

AI features in UDAYRA are designed to assist users in understanding data and process flow. AI does not replace professional judgment.

What AI Does in UDAYRA

  • Explains GST figures in simple, easy-to-understand language
  • Explains invoice reading status (Ready / Pending / Returned)
  • Identifies if documents / invoices need to be uploaded
  • Provides tentative explanation work for Chartered Accountants

What AI Does Not Do

  • Does not calculate or decide tax liability
  • Does not determine ITC eligibility
  • Does not validate GST compliance
  • Does not access or log in to the GST portal
  • Does not modify accounting or invoice data

Professional Responsibility

AI-generated outputs in UDAYRA are provided for explanation only.
Final GST return filing is always performed by a qualified Chartered Accountant.
All statutory decisions and filings remain the responsibility of the CA.
